NMAP Script

nmap -sV -p 111 --script=rpcinfo 10.11.1.1-254

nmap -p 111 --script nfs* 10.11.1.72

Mount NFS

mkdir john

Sudo mount -o nolock 10.1.1.72:/home/john john
