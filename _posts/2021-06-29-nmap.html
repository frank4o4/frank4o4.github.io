NMAP

 

Scan all ports and output to file

nmap -p- -sC -sV -o nmap.out 

 

 

TCP Scan all ports

sudo nmap -sC -sS -p0-65535

 

UDP Scanning top 20 ports

nmap -sU --top-ports 20

UDP

Sudo nmap -sS -sU

 

Quick Scan

nmap -sT -p- --min-rate 5000

 

 

UDP Scan

sudo nmap --top-ports 100 -sU -o udp.scan
